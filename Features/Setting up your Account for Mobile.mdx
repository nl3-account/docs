### Using the App
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion
    title="Notifications"
    icon="bell">
    Notifications are managed in the Notifications pane.
    - **Account Unlock Request** - When an account is locked, users will receive a notification to Approve/Reject unlocking the account. Unlock requests inform the users about the location the request originated from as well as the account and application requested to be unlocked.
    <img src="C:\Users\18649\Documents\Career\nl3\mobile app - iOS\walkthrough\apple\functionality\placeit\Apple - Notification - Account Unlock Request.png" />
    - **Device Registration Request** - When a user registers a new device with Next Level3, they must first approve adding that device by accepting an Add Device request. This request must be approved before the new device is added to the users account.
    <img src="C:\Users\18649\Documents\Career\nl3\mobile app - iOS\walkthrough\apple\functionality\placeit\Apple - Notification - Device Registration Request.png" />
    - **Secondary Approval Request** - Secondary Approval Unlock Requests are sent to users who are listed as the secondary approver for the multi-approval policies. If an account is associated to a multi-approval policy, all secondary approvers are sent a Account Unlock Request after the primary approver has approved unlocking the account.
    <img src="C:\Users\18649\Documents\Career\nl3\mobile app - iOS\walkthrough\apple\functionality\placeit\Apple - Notification - Secondary Approval Request.png" />
</Accordion>
<Accordion
    title="Accounts"

</Accordion>

We reccomend leaving this switch turned on at all times. The "Relock Any Unlocked Accounts After" setting allows you to manage the amount of time you have to access your account before it automatically locks itself.

        </Tab>
        <Tab
        title="Support"

        <img src="C:\Users\18649\Documents\Career\nl3\mobile app - iOS\walkthrough\apple\functionality\placeit\Apple - Settings - Support.png" />
        </Tab>
        <Tab
        title="Licensing"
        icon="id-badge">
        The Licensing setting allows you to view the End User License agreement. Once you agree to the the terms and conditions you can tap the support icon in the bottom right corner to send us a message and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
        <img src="C:\Users\18649\Documents\Career\nl3\mobile app - iOS\walkthrough\apple\functionality\placeit\Apple - Settings - License.png" />
        <img src="C:\Users\18649\Documents\Career\nl3\mobile app - iOS\walkthrough\apple\functionality\placeit\Apple - Settings - License Support.png" />
        </Tab>

        title="Logout"
        icon="right-from-bracket">
        The Logout setting is an option used when you are ready to logout of the app. Once you tap the option to logout you will be logged out of your account.
